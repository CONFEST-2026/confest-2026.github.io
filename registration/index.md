#### What's included?
 - The Full Ticket includes access to all conferences and workshops throughout the week, lunch and coffee/snacks, and all social events. 
 - Workshop Only covers attendance, lunch and coffee/snacks for the workshops on Saturday only. Participants are free to switch between workshops.


#### Registration Deadline

Participants are strongly encouraged to register before the early registration deadline on 31 July 2026. Registration will remain open until after the event at a higher price.


## Funding support

Limited funding will be available to support students, early career researchers and others who would otherwise not be able to attend without support.

The funding takes the form of fee waivers and/or possibly travel support.

Students and Early career research making use of these funds are encouraged to participate in [YR-Concur](/workshops/yr-concur/)

Those interested in applying for these funds should send an email to [confest2026@liverpool.ac.uk](mailto:confest2026@liverpool.ac.uk) before **1 July 2026**, notifications will be provided by **5 July 2026**.
