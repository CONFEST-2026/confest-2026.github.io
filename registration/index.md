#### What's included?
 - The Full Ticket includes access to all three conferences, all workshops, and all social events. 
 - Workshop Only covers all workshops taking place on Saturday 5 September only. Participants are free to switch between workshops. Participants with a Full Ticket do not need this.


#### Registration Deadline

Participants are strongly encouraged to register before the early registration deadline on 31 July 2026. Registrations occurring after this are more expensive.


## Funding support

Limited funding is expected to be available to support students, early career researchers and others who would otherwise not be able to attend without support.

The funding takes the form of fee waivers and/or possibly travel support.

Students and Early career research making use of these funds are strongly encouraged to participate in [YR-Concur](/workshops/yr-concur/)

Those interested in applying for these funds should send an email to [confest2026@liverpool.ac.uk](mailto:confest2026@liverpool.ac.uk) before **1 July 2026**, notifications will be provided by **5 July 2026**.
