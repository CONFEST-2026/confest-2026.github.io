#### What's included?
 - The Full Ticket includes Conference and Workshops, and access to all social events. 
 - Workshop only ticket covers activities on Saturday 5 September only.


#### Registration Deadline

Participants are strongly encouraged to register before the early registration deadline on 31 July 2026. Registrations occurring after this will are more expensive.


## Funding support

Limited funds is expected to be available to support students, early career researchers and other who would otherwise not be able to attend without support.

The funding takes the form of fee waivers and/or possibly travel support.

Students and Early career research making use of these funds are strongly encouraged to participate in [YR-Concur](/workshops/yr-concur/)

Those interested in applying for these funds should send an email to [confest2026@liverpool.ac.uk](mailto:confest2026@liverpool.ac.uk)
