# CONFEST 2026 Call for Participation


Dear Colleagues,

We are excited to invite you to register for CONFEST 2026, which will host three major international conferences:

- CONCUR 2026, the 37th International Conference on Concurrency Theory
- QEST+FORMATS 2026, the 3rd joint International Conference on Quantitative Evaluation of SysTems and on Formal Modeling and Analysis of Timed Systems
- FMICS 2026, the 31st International Conference on Formal Methods for Industrial Critical Systems

These events will take place in Liverpool, UK, from September 1 to September 5.

### Registration

The Early Registration deadline is July 31, 2026:
<https://confest-2026.github.io/registration/>

Information on accommodation, including on-campus accommodation and conference
hotel rates: <https://confest-2026.github.io/local/accommodation>

The workshops Express/SOS, SNR, and YR-Concur are still accepting submissions:
<https://expresssos.github.io/conf/2026>
<https://confest-2026.github.io/workshops/snr/>
<https://confest-2026.github.io/workshops/yr-concur/>
